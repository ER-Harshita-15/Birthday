## Quick Setup Steps

1. Create folder: `public/images/`
2. Add your photos with the names listed above
3. Or rename the image references in `/src/app/components/BookPages.tsx`

## Image Tips

- **Recommended Size**: 1000px - 2000px width
- **Format**: JPG or PNG
- **Orientation**: Portrait works best for most layouts
- **Optimize**: Compress images to reduce file size

---

**Pro Tip**: You can use different filenames! Just update the paths in `BookPages.tsx` to match your actual image names.
